About Me

As a writer drawn to interactive storytelling, I wear many hats. I combine my background in journalism and technical writing with a growing interest in frontend development to create compelling, visual narratives at the intersection of data, design, and storytelling.


I'm especially interested in projects that deepen public understanding of complex topics such as gender issues, climate change, and social equity. Through scrollytelling, maps, and charts, I aim to build experiences that inform, engage, and inspire readers.

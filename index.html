About Me

As a writer passionate about interactive storytelling, I wear many hats as a developer, designer, and communicator. With a foundation in journalism and technical writing, I've developed a deep respect for clarity, accuracy, and narrative structure. Now, as I expand into programming and data storytelling, I'm drawn to the creative possibilities that emerge when code meets story. I focus on building immersive, visually rich narratives that sit at the crossroads of data, design, and user experience.


My work is driven by a desire to make complex topics more accessible and engaging, particularly around themes like gender equity, climate change, and social justice. Whether it's through scrollytelling, dynamic maps, or data-driven charts, I aim to craft experiences that not only inform but also resonate—sparking curiosity, empathy, and action in the reader.
